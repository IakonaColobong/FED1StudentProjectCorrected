Class Subjects

Japanese Cuisine

Taught by reknown Japanese Chef Masayoshi Kazato, you will learn the intricacies and traditions of delicate Japanese flavors.

Hawaiian Cuisine

Chef Roy Yamaguchi will take students through the history and preperation styles of this unique cuisine as they blend various meats and fish to form mouth watering flavors.

Indian Cuisine

Chef Sanjeev Mehrota will guide students into the sull flavors and spices Indian Cuisine is reknown for.

Greek Cuisine

When it comes to giving people that warm fire and comfortable dining feel of traditionally prepared food, it is hard to beat Greek Cuisine. Chef Lefteris Lazarou will help students bring this comfortable feeling to their customers as he teaches new chefs the most popular foods and traditional dishes.

French Cuisine

Long respected for its ability to enhance any dining experience, French Cuisine is a staple for many aspiring chefs to tackle. Chef Lee Anne Wong will guide students through their first experiences in the popular style.

Pastry

As much science as it is an art, Pastry Chef Caherine Nielsen instructs students in the fine art of Pastry making. From the perfect measurements of ingredients to the decoration of international pastries, she will ensure student recieve a solid foundation in this complex art.
